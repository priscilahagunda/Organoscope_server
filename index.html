Who we are

Organo scope Ltd is a startup founded and run by a group of professional Kenyan women based in Kenya and the diaspora. Organo scope limited breeds Black Soldier Fly(BSF) to consume and convert organic waste into products such as animal feed and fertilizer.

Our activities

  1. Raising BSF to produce animal feed and organic fertilizers.
  2. Training women, youth and small-scale farmers to raise BSF as a source of income.
  3. Pre-identified organic waste collection and management.

Mission and vision

To turn waste reliably and consistently into value.
To reduce dependency on Omena (silver cyprinid) as the principal source of animal protein in the animal feed value chain.

our services

What we do

BSF larvae breeding

Breeding of BSF and using them to produce animal proteins to supply consumers in Kenya and eventually throughout East Africa and the world.

Organic waste collection and management

Collection of organic food waste from local supermarkets, food-based factories, farms and restaurants. 

Organic animal feed production

Production and selling BSF larvae as feeds for cattle, pigs and poultry.

Organic Fertilizer production

Production of organic soil fertilizers.  

Organic Farming

Farming using our organic soil fertilizers made with BSF.  

Poultry Farming

Chicken rearing which are fed with our BSF larvae.  

Training

Empowerment of women, youth, and small-scale farmers by providing training in raising BSF and creation of opportunities for them to earn income and access markets for their products.

Learn more about BSF farming

  • What is BSF?

    BSF(Black Soldier Fly) are the type of flies that are slightly bigger than the normal flies and are known for their extraordinary ability to consume and compost waste into animal feed. It is a non-pest insect species that can convert diverse (non-value) organic waste streams into biomass which can then be used to produce animal feed.

  • Why BSF?

    Excellent source of protein. At larvae stage BSF can transform organic waste into high quality protein which can be used as alternative protein additive in animal feed.

  • Our products

    • Organo meal
      This is a feed containing 55% protein which can be used for Chickens, fish and Pets (monogastric animals).
    • Organo oil
      This is a pure natural oil with high lauric acid concentration which is used as aquaculture feed, pet food and alternative to fish, coconut and palm oil.
    • Organo soil
      This is an organic soil conditioner prepared from larvae residue containing high nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium which are essential in soil fertility.

  • What is Organo Scope Ltd's goal?

    Produce both animal feed and fertilizer in sustainable quantities that will impact affordability and accesability by the breeding of BSF using organic waste products.
